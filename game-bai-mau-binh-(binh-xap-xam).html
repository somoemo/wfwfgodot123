Luat choi

Muc tieu cua tro choi la sap xep 3 chi sao cho chi ba la co tong diem lon hon hoac bang tong diem cua chi doi va chi co. Neu dat duoc dieu nay, nguoi choi se thang cuoc.



Diem so trong Mau Binh duoc tinh theo thu tu bai: At (1 diem), 2-9 (tuong ung voi so tren bai), 10, J, Q, K (tuong ung voi 10 diem). Diem so cua mot van choi se duoc tinh bang tong diem cua 3 chi.

Cach choi

Trong qua trinh choi, nguoi choi co the doi cac la bai trong chi co va chi doi de tang co hoi thang cuoc. Tuy nhien, chi co the doi bai khi tong diem cua 3 chi chua vuot qua 13 diem.
